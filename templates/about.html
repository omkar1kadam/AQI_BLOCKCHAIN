About Us

Decentralized. Data-Driven. Earth-First.

At Plentera, we believe that small actions can spark global change. Our mission is to empower individuals to monitor environmental health using low-cost sensor kits that track Air Quality, Temperature, and Humidity in real time.

We combine IoT and blockchain technology to create a transparent, decentralized network that rewards users for contributing meaningful environmental data. With every data point, we get closer to smarter pollution control, improved agricultural planning, and a healthier planet — together.
